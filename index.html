Efficacy

End-to-end trials measuring the effectiveness of both biological and chemical crop protection products.

Crop Selectivity

Bio-Stimulus

Assessing products that enhance plant health, improve stress resilience, and stimulate growth.

Plant Growth Regulator

Testing regulators to optimize development stages, control flowering & fruiting, and improve crop uniformity & productivity.
